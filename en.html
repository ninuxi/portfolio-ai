About

Creative Technologist and Audio AI Engineer with experience in interactive systems for installations, theatre and brands. I design solutions that merge computer vision, audio AI, and integration with leading live software and hardware (QLab, Resolume Arena, Ableton Live, grandMA, TouchDesigner, Chamsys...) to enable real-time reactive experiences.

Over the years I contributed to projects for Triennale Milano, D&D Group, Auditorium Parco della Musica, Cartier, Mulino Bianco, as well as multiple theatres and cultural institutions.

Antonio Mainenti (Sicily, 1978) is a Sound Engineer and Designer with a strong background as a musician. His research integrates embedded systems development (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32) with the creation of complex multisensory installations. Specialized in applying artificial intelligence to sound, he uses frameworks such as TensorFlow, Magenta, and MusicLM to build generative models that merge audio and visual storytelling. Expert in immersive audio (L-ISA, Dolby Atmos), he designs three-dimensional environments by leveraging AI to redefine the boundaries of algorithmic composition and sonic interactivity.
