[cite: 2] [cite_start]Il sistema offre una soluzione avanzata per superare la natura statica degli allestimenti tradizionali, abilitando la creazione di ambienti che si modificano dinamicamente in base a condizioni reali e misurabili. Functional Architecture of the System [cite: 43]

[cite_start]

MOOD operates through three main integrated modules: [cite: 44]

1. [cite_start]Environmental Analysis Engine (AI-Powered) [cite: 45]

    [cite_start]
  • Computer Vision: Through cameras, the engine performs real-time audience analysis, quantifying aggregate parameters such as crowd density, people count, and kinetic energy (motion) level. [cite: 46] [cite_start]The system is designed to ensure privacy, analyzing data anonymously to perceive the general atmosphere without identifying individuals. [cite: 47]

  • Audio Analysis: Using ambient microphones, the system analyzes the soundscape to classify it. [cite: 48] [cite_start]It distinguishes between silence, background buzz, conversations and music, measuring metrics such as volume (dB) and spectral energy to enrich AI decision context. [cite: 49]

MOOD acts as an orchestra conductor, interfacing fluently with industry-standard professional software. [cite: 51] [cite_start]The current implementation includes native control of: [cite: 52]

The system architecture is inherently modular and scalable. [cite: 56] [cite_start]Integration of additional software (e.g., TouchDesigner, Ableton Live) or communication protocols (MIDI, DMX) is native, ensuring longevity and adaptability of the investment. [cite: 57]
